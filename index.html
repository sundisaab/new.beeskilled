Frequently Asked Questions

What is the duration of the courses?

Most courses are 8–12 weeks long, designed to be self-paced with weekly live doubt sessions to ensure flexibility and thorough learning.

Can I change my course after enrollment?

Yes, we understand preferences change. You can request a course switch within the first week of enrollment by contacting our support team.

How many projects will I get?

Our curriculum focuses on hands-on learning. You will work on at least 2 minor projects and 1 major industry-grade capstone project during the course.

Are the courses suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! We start from the very basics and gradually guide you to advanced concepts. No prior coding experience is mandatory for beginner tracks.

What kind of projects will I work on?

You will build real-world applications relevant to your field, such as e-commerce websites, AI prediction models, or data analytics dashboards.

Do you provide certificates upon completion?

Yes, upon successful completion, you receive an AICTE & MSME recognized certificate that is verifiable and adds significant value to your resume.
